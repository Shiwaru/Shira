Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Lúmina Project

1. Introduction

Shira Launcher ("the App") is developed by Lúmina Project. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Shira Launcher does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

3. Local Data Storage

All data generated by the App (game instances, configuration, downloaded files) is stored locally on your device inside the ShiraData folder. This data never leaves your computer.

4. Microsoft Authentication

If you choose to log in with a Microsoft account (Premium accounts), authentication is handled entirely through Microsoft's official OAuth services. Shira Launcher does not store your Microsoft credentials. Tokens are stored locally using Windows DPAPI encryption and are never transmitted to Lúmina Project servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Shira Launcher communicates with the following third-party services to provide its core functionality:

— Mojang/Microsoft servers: to download Minecraft game files and authenticate premium accounts.

— Modrinth and CurseForge APIs: to browse and download mods (when using the mod browser feature).

— MineSkin API: to upload and apply custom skins.

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data handling.

6. Children's Privacy

Shira Launcher does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App does not include any features designed to collect personal information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the Shira Launcher GitHub repository.
